General Notes:

  • Turn-around times are approximately 15-20 minutes for dine-in and take-out orders, 30-50 minutes for deliveries.
  • All delivery and take-out orders must be placed before 10:30 PM.
  • All dine-in orders must be placed before 10:15 PM.
  • Orders placed after the cut-off time will be processed when our kitchen opens (10:30 AM) on the next day.
  • Our busiest hours for dine-in customers are Thursday, Friday and Saturday nights from 5 PM to closing. Seating is first-come first-served at all times.
  • Our delivery zip codes are 98107, 98117, 98103, 98199, 98119, and 98109. No exceptions!
  • All build-your-own pizzas include a complimentary topping. Additional toppings are $1.00
  • We will be glad to take off, or make a 1 for 1 substitution of, any of our ingredients on our speciality pizzas (discounts not given for subtracted ingredients). Please use the comment section for this request.
  • You may add any additional toppings to our house specalties for the normal additional topping fee.
  • Vegan cheese and gluten free crust are a $2.00 surcharge each and neither are eligible as a complimentary or 1 for 1 topping. Vegan cheese is automatically included on our vegan specality.
