Sunshine Boat Rentals and Charters

Rental rates for all boats

Daily - $15 per linear foot
3 to 6 days - $45 per linear foot
7 to 10 days - $60 per linear foot

Includes use of tie up slip. Long-term rentals are available (please inquire with the sales staff on rates and availability). Rentals during the off-season are automatically discounted 20 percent.

Optional rental insurance
$20 per day or $100 per week. $500 deductible on damages.
Note: liability insurance is required for all rentals. Our optional rental insurance satisfies this requirement.

Readiness Deposit
$10 per linear foot, plus cost of fuel if returned with less than a full tank. Readiness deposit is fully refundable if the boat is properly cleaned, holding tank pumped out, and completely refueled before being returned.

Chartering

We offer full service chartering at double the rental rates. Readiness deposit and insurance is not required. Availability is limited, particularly during the off-season. Please inquire with the sales staff.
