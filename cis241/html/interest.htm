Interests

Like I mentioned on the main page for my site, I enjoy travelling, fishing, hunting, skiing, riding bicycles, working out, sail and the list goes on. Below are some links to some websites that correspond to my interests:

  • Kayak.com - It's a search engine for finding the lowest possible cost on flights, hotels, vacations and rental cars. If you want to travel as cheap as possible, Kayak is the way to go.
  • E-Dreams - Functions the same as Kayak, but is based in Europe and can sometimes beat Kayak out in finding the lowest price.
  • BUG Europe - If you want to figure out how to travel Europe on a budget, this site can help you figure everything out.
  • Hostel Bookers - Travelling on the cheap almost inevitably means staying in a hostel. Hostel Bookers can not only help you book your hostel, but can also give you ratings and reviews.
  • Washington State Department of Fish & Wildlife - Before you go fishing or hunting, you need to get your appropriate licenses and endorsements. The WDFW's website will help you do that and is also an excellent resource for learning about some of the better fishing and hunting areas.
  • Skiing and Snowboarding in Washington - This site has a good list of all of Washington's skiing and snowboarding destinations.
