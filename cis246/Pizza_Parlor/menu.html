Seattle's Premier Organic Pizza Experience

Ordering at Wholesome Pizza is easy. Each pizza size has a base price. Each house speciality price is added to the base price. Vegan cheese and gluten free crust options are $2.00 each. Every other topping is $1.00, but the frist topping on a custom pizza is free. We are happy to include extra toppings on custom pizzas for the regular price. We also welcome 1-for-1 substitutions and subtractions of any topping on our house specialities*.

*Vegan cheese and gluten free crust are not elibible as complimentary toppings or any kind of substitution.

House Specialities

Meat Lovers: + $2.50
Our meat packed pizza includes pepperoni, sausage, chicken suasage, salumi, and prosciutto. Guarenteed to satiate any carnivorous craving.

Greek: + $3.00
A Mediterranean classic that is one of our most popular pizzas. Includes spinach, feta cheese, onions, tomatoes, garlic and chicken.

Seafood Special: + $3.50
The catch of the day on a pizza. Includes locally sourced clams and shrimp coupled with spinach, garlic, artichokes and capers.

American Supreme: + $3.50
Includes pepperoni, sausage, salumi, olives, onions, mushrooms and tomatoes. A good all around pizza guarenteed to please.

Vegeterian Delight: + $3.00
Our vegetarian counterpart to our meatlovers pizza. Includes olives, spinach, garlic, zuchinni, tomatoes and artichokes.

Vegan Delight: + $4.00
The same toppings as our vegetarian delight, but with all vegan products. No extra cost required.

Super Canadian Bacon: + $2.00
Double doses of cheese, Canadian bacon, and pineapple. If you're a Canadian bacon fan, this one is a must.
